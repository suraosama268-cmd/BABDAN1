ABOUT THE WEB

Description of the Babadan website for transportation within Baghdad About Babdan:
"Babadan" is a website specialized in providing modern internal transportation services within the capital, Baghdad. It aims to facilitate movement between the different areas of Baghdad through an integrated network of stations and transport lines, while providing easy electronic reservation services based on the latest technologies.

Service Details

1. Number of Stations:

Babadan has 30 stations distributed throughout Baghdad, taking into account population density and local needs. The stations were distributed in a balanced manner between the two sides of Baghdad:

  • 15 stations on the Karkh side.
  • 15 stations on the Rusafa side.

2. Number of Lines:

12 main transmission lines are operated to cover all areas:

  • 6 lines in Karkh: covering major residential and commercial areas.
  • 6 lines in Rusafa: serving high-density neighborhoods and vital areas.

3. Working Hours:

Flights run daily from 5:00 am to 1:00 am, ensuring that the mobility needs of all groups are covered, whether for work, study, or leisure.

4. Ticket Types and Prices:

  • Daily ticket: 3,000 IQD.
  • Weekly ticket: 18,000 IQD.
  • Monthly ticket: 65,000 IQD.
  • Annual pass: competitively priced with discounts for regular subscribers (price breakdown shown when subscribing).

5. Online Booking Service:

  • Bookings are made entirely online using credit cards.
  • The user gets a QR code after booking confirmation.
  • Upon arrival at the station, the code is scanned via dedicated QR recognition devices to activate the trip.

6. Subscription Options:

  • Daily bookings for individuals who use the service irregularly.
  • Weekly, monthly, and yearly subscriptions suitable for students and employees who need daily commuting.

Additional Services

1. Passenger comfort:

All buses are air-conditioned, with comfortable seats and free internet access during the flight.

2. Security Coverage:

Equipped with surveillance cameras to ensure the safety of passengers throughout the trip.

3. Special Offers:

• Discounts on monthly and annual tickets during festive seasons.
• Exclusive offers for students and staff.

4. Customer Service:

Babadan provides a support team available around the clock to answer queries and resolve problems.

The bottom line

Babadan aims to provide a modern and safe service that suits the needs of Baghdad residents, ensuring an easy and efficient transportation experience for all.
